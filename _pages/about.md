I received my Ph.D. from Montana State University in 2020 under supervision of Dr. Indika Kahanda. My research lies in the area of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning for extracting information from unstructured text. Particularly, I apply Transformers, RNNs, CNNs, and classic machine learning algorithms to a variety of NLP tasks including, named entity recognition, relation extraction, and question answering.
