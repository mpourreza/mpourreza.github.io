## 1. Summary

This app helps you read poetry attributed to Hafez and take a traditional “faal” by choosing a poem at random from text stored **inside the app**. We designed the app so that **we do not ask you to create an account** and **we do not intentionally collect personal information** through the app for our own use.

## 2. Information the app processes

### Content you see in the app

Poem text and related presentation data are **included in the app** when you install it. Opening poems or using the faal feature does **not** send your choices to our servers in the current version of the app, because the app does not use our backend for that.

### Information we do not intentionally collect

In the current version, we do **not** intentionally collect:

- Your name, email, or phone number  
- Precise location  
- Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera  
- Advertising IDs for cross-app tracking  

If this changes in a future update, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for consent or provide other legally required notices.
