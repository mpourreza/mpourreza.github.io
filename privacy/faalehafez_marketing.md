# Faal-e-Hafez

**Hafez, on your phone — read the ghazals and take a faal whenever you like.**
Hafez is a calm, focused app for exploring the poetry attributed to **Hafez (حافظ)**. Browse the full collection, open any ghazal for quiet reading, or use the traditional **faal** ritual: ask your heart’s question, tap once, and let the app open a poem at random from the text bundled inside the app.

No accounts. No feeds. Just the verses.

---

## Why this app

- **Complete catalog** — Scroll through the poems included with the app and open any one instantly.
- **Authentic faal experience** — A simple, respectful flow: intention → brief pause → a randomly chosen ghazal.
- **Made for Persian** — Right‑to‑left layout and typography tuned for comfortable reading.
- **Works offline** — Poem text lives in the app; read and take faal without a network connection for the poetry itself.
- **Dark, easy on the eyes** — Interface designed for reading in low light.

---

## Who it’s for

- Anyone who already loves Hafez and wants a **dedicated** place for his ghazals on iOS (and Android, when published).
- People who use **faal** as reflection or tradition — not as fortune‑telling science, but as a moment to sit with a poem.

---

## Privacy

We keep the app simple: **we don’t run a login or social graph in the app**, and the poetry you read is not sent to our servers for the core experience. Details: https://imorteza.com/privacy/faalehafez

---

## About the text

The app includes poetry **attributed to Hafez**. Edition, numbering, and small textual choices may differ from printed books you already own; that’s normal across sources.
