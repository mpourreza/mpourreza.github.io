## Quick fixes

### The app won’t open or crashes on launch

1. **Force quit** the app and open it again.  
2. **Restart** your phone.  
3. Check for an **update** on the App Store / Play Store.  
4. If it still fails: email us with your **device model**, **OS version**, and **app version** (Settings → General → About → Version, or the app’s version from the store listing).

### Poems don’t load or the list is empty

The catalog is **bundled inside the app**. If the list is empty after install:

1. **Update** to the latest version from the store.  
2. If the problem remains, contact us — include a **screenshot** if possible.

### Faal button does nothing

The faal flow waits a short moment on purpose. Make sure you’re **online only if your build requires it** (the standard design is offline for text). If the button stays disabled, the app may still be loading fonts or data: wait a few seconds, then try again.

### Text looks cut off or the wrong font

Tell us your **device**, **OS version**, and whether you use **Display Zoom** or large accessibility text sizes. We use readable defaults, but extreme sizes can affect layout.

### RTL / Persian display issues

Describe what looks wrong (which screen, which line), and attach a **screenshot**. Mention if you use a **right‑to‑left system language** or mixed LTR/RTL settings.
